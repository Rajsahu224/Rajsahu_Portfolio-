About Me

A young filmmaker driven by emotion and visual storytelling — learning with every frame, growing with every story.

My name is Bhanu Raj Sahu, and I'm currently studying Television Programme and Filmmaking at RUDN University in Moscow. Originally from India, I bring a diverse cultural perspective to my work. I create films that aim not only to entertain but also to inspire viewers to question, reflect, and think deeply about the world around them.
